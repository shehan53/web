Cat carriers are small portable boxes, crates, or cages used to transport cats from one location to another. The two main types are the front openers (these are generally tough plastic boxes with a metal door, such as cat crates) and top openers (these are generally more like cages with a hinged roof), although there are other types. Cat bowls should be elevated if you have a cat with joint issues or trouble bending down to eat. Some cats with digestive issues may benefit from elevated bowls, too, and many healthy cats simply prefer them. Talk to your vet about whether an elevated feeding station or bowl may benefit your cat.
